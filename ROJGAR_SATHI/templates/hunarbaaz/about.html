About Rozgaar Saathi

Rozgaar Saathi is a community-driven platform created to connect skilled labourers, known as "Hunarbaaz", with clients seeking reliable, verified service providers in their local area.

Whether you're a carpenter, electrician, plumber, or construction worker, Rozgaar Saathi offers a way to build a digital presence, showcase your work, and receive job requests without needing advanced tech skills.

For clients, it's a reliable source to search, filter, and connect with verified professionals quickly and transparently, based on skills, location, and reviews.

Our Vision

To bridge the digital divide and empower rural and semi-urban skilled workers by bringing their services online in a dignified, accessible, and trusted manner.

Our Mission

We aim to provide simple tools to skilled workers and local clients, fostering trust, transparency, and growth through technology.
