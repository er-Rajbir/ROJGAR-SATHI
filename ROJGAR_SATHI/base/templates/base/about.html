About Rozgaar Saathi

Rozgaar Saathi is a people-first, community-powered platform designed to bridge the gap between skilled labourers — "Hunarbaaz" — and clients in need of dependable, verified local services.

Our Vision

At Rozgaar Saathi, we envision an inclusive local economy where every skilled worker in Amritsar — from city centre to colonies — can access job opportunities through the power of technology.

Our goal is to ensure that no electrician, painter, plumber, or construction worker is left behind simply for lacking digital presence. We empower them with tools suited to their pace and needs.

With Amritsar as our starting point, we aim to build a strong local network that bridges the gap between verified Hunarbaaz and clients — one neighborhood at a time.

Our Mission

Rozgaar Saathi was born from a powerful mission — to connect skilled workers in Amritsar with real, local work, using reliable and simple tech.

  • Local Inclusion: Easy digital profiles for Hunarbaaz across Amritsar — no matter their background.
  • Trust & Transparency: Verified profiles, honest reviews, and visible work history for every hiring.
  • Community Growth: Empowering workers, supporting families, and strengthening local relationships.

We’re not just offering work — we’re offering dignity, respect, and recognition for Amritsar’s skilled workforce.

Our Values

Our values guide everything — from design to support:

Empathy: We understand real challenges and offer simple, inclusive solutions.

Trust: With verification, transparency, and secure communication, both parties feel confident.

Empowerment: We help Hunarbaaz grow professionally by offering visibility and credibility.

Community: We’re building a fair, respectful platform where everyone can thrive — together.
