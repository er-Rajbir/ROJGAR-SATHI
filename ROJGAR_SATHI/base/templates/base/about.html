About Rozgaar Saathi

Bringing skills and services together.

Rozgaar Saathi is a people-first, community-powered platform designed to bridge the gap between skilled labourers — known as "Hunarbaaz" — and clients in need of dependable, verified local services.

Whether you're an electrician, plumber, painter, carpenter, or daily-wage worker, Rozgaar Saathi provides you with a digital identity, a platform to showcase your work, and access to real job opportunities — all without needing complex tech knowledge.

For clients, it’s a reliable space to search, compare, and directly connect with local service providers. With features like verified profiles, work samples, and community reviews, hiring becomes faster, safer, and more transparent.

Our Vision

At Rozgaar Saathi, we envision an inclusive local economy where every skilled worker in Amritsar — whether from the bustling city centre or nearby mohallas and colonies — can gain equal access to job opportunities through the power of technology.

Our goal is to ensure that no electrician, painter, plumber, or construction worker is left behind simply because they don’t have a digital identity. We aim to uplift these individuals by offering them a platform that is made for them — in their language, at their pace, and with their realities in mind.

With Amritsar as our starting point, we are committed to building a strong and trusted local network that bridges the gap between verified Hunarbaaz and clients in need — one neighbourhood at a time.

Our Mission

Rozgaar Saathi was born out of a simple but powerful mission — to connect skilled workers in Amritsar with real, local work opportunities, using simple and reliable technology.

  • Local Inclusion: We make it easy for Hunarbaaz across Amritsar to register, create digital profiles, and showcase their skills to potential clients — ensuring access to opportunities regardless of their background or location within the city.
  • Trust & Transparency: Every job request, rating, and review builds trust in the local community. Clients can see verified profiles, past work, and honest feedback before hiring — ensuring confidence in every booking.
  • Community Growth: We believe in growing together. By supporting the hardworking Hunarbaaz of Amritsar, we also uplift local families, create job security, and build long-lasting relationships within the city.

Our mission is not just to provide work — but to give respect, dignity, and recognition to the backbone of Amritsar’s daily life: its skilled workforce.
